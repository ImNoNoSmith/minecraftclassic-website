PPF (Preprints in Particles and Fields) is a list of high-energy physics preprints received during the previous week at the SLAC Library. To obtain a copy of an item on this list, check your own preprint library or write directly to the authors. For b-board papers you may also download a TeX version from a bulletin board, or get a postscript version from the SLAC's anonymous ftp server, preprint.slac.stanford.edu

'Print' and RX report numbers are assigned by SLAC to unnumbered preprints and should not be used in requests or references. DO NOT REQUEST PREPRINTS FROM SLAC, except, of course, those by SLAC authors. The PPF Source Address List is available free on request. You may also use the INSTITUTIONS and HEPNAMES databases to find authors' addresses. (Genuine SLAC preprints may be requested from authors or from the SLAC Publication Department, techpub@slacvm.slac.stanford.edu )

Many preprints are now available from various bulletin boards on the Internet. They are identified by the bulletin board name and number. For instructions and a list of bulletin boards, send e-mail to hepth@xxx.lanl.gov , with Subject: HELP . For bulletin board papers in this list, you may display abstracts by choosing appropriate Abstract_and_Paper boxes. When you open an abstract, you are given further options. If you are accessing WWW via an X browser, such as MidasWWW or Xmosaic, you may be able to view the entire paper by clicking on Show_Paper. Do not use this option with a line browser or a browser that does not handle postscript. Instead, get the postscript file of the already TeX-ed paper from the SLAC's anonymous ftp server and print it on your own postscript printer, or obtain the TeX source from the appropriate bulletin board and process it yourself. You can view the TeX source by displaying the abstract and then clicking on Show_TeX_Source.
